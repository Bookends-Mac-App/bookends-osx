## What is Bookends?  

**Bookends** is a powerful **research management and citation tool** designed to simplify how academics, students, and professionals organize references and bibliographies. It combines a modern interface with advanced automation to make collecting, citing, and formatting references effortless.  

With Bookends, users can import citations directly from online databases such as PubMed, JSTOR, and Google Scholar. The software automatically downloads bibliographic data, full-text PDFs, and metadata, keeping your research library fully synchronized and searchable.  

Its smart linking and **live citation feature** allow users to insert properly formatted references into documents in real time, working smoothly with Pages, Word, and Mellel. You can annotate PDFs, highlight key passages, and even tag or group papers by topic or author for easy access.  

Built natively for macOS, Bookends offers seamless performance and iCloud synchronization, making it ideal for both individual researchers and collaborative teams who need consistent, reliable reference control.
